About

In the winter of 2009, I took a cake decorating course, but having been diagnosed with Celiac disease, and only eating gluten free food, I thought I could bake and decorate regular cakes and serve them to my family and friends to enjoy – the creative part was the fun part for me! Then I started to experiment with family recipes, making them gluten free.

I took my cake decorating skills and my gluten-free lifestyle and developed an array of fabulous desserts that you wouldn't be able to tell are gluten-free – desserts and entrees that not only my friends and family would love but, that being Celiac, I could enjoy as well.

Everyone deserves a little decadence in their busy life. Gluten-free food has come a long way in the last 20 years but still those who have Celiac or are gluten-intolerant or simply want to avoid gluten still have to go into the freezer section to purchase desserts. My mission from the beginning was not only to develop a great tasting collection of desserts and entrees that were gluten-free, but also were FRESH (not frozen), were made from scratch with high quality ingredients, contained no preservatives, artificial flavourings, colouring, fillers or additives.
